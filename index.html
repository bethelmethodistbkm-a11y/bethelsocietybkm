Who We Are

A Community Rooted in Wesleyan Faith

Bethel Society – Jamdede is a vibrant congregation within The Methodist Church Ghana, proudly serving under the Sunyani Diocese and the Berekum Circuit. We are a community of believers committed to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service in the love of Christ.

Founded on the Wesleyan tradition, our society embraces the values of social holiness — loving God and loving neighbour — as the heartbeat of everything we do.

  • ✦ Authentic Worship rooted in Scripture and Tradition
  • ✦ Radical Hospitality — all are welcome at Bethel
  • ✦ Servant Leadership in the Berekum community
  • ✦ Nurturing families and youth for God's Kingdom
  • ✦ Committed to the Methodist Church Ghana's vision
Our Mission

Making Disciples of Jesus Christ

We are called to make, mature, and mobilise disciples of Jesus Christ for the transformation of our community and the world. Every programme, every gathering, and every outreach at Bethel is designed with this single purpose in mind.

Our Vision

To be a Spirit-filled, biblically-grounded, and community-transforming church that reflects the love and holiness of God in all we do — here in Jamdede and beyond.
